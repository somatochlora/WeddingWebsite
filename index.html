Things to Know

Head's up - you won't actually see us get married!

Emily has always dreamed of getting married at city hall - so we are officially tying the knot at London City Hall in advance. But we also really want to share this special moment with you and the rest of our loved ones!

Kids are absolutely welcome!

We'll have lots of kid-friendly food and activities, and lots of room to run and play and nap in the shade.

An invite to wear your best linen, seersucker or florals!

TThere is no dress code - please wear what you'll find comfortable! We'll be wearing our best garden party looks - big hats and fascinators welcome!

Come hungry!

We're planning a great spread of all our garden party favourites and there will be something for everyone! We will also be serving coffee, tea, soft drinks and water. Let us know in the survey if you have any dietary restrictions we should keep in mind.

Ready for a party?

Us too! We can't serve alcohol in the park, so we're hosting an afterparty (details to be announced, it will be accessible and suitable for all ages). Let us know in the survey if you're interested in attending and we'll follow up with more info.

Things you might like to bring:

You don't need to bring anything, but you may want to bring:

  • Sunscreen and/or a sunhat
  • A lawn chair or picnic blanket (there are picnic tables but this will allow you to spread out)
  • binoculars (for all the birders out there)

Please take lots of photos!

Activities

Want some ideas for things to do around St. Thomas and Southern London? Here are some suggestions:

St. Thomas and area:

  • The St. Thomas Elevated Park for interactive art installations and a panoramic view of the area.
  • Don't forget to check out the Jumbo the Elephant Memorial Statue!
  • Visit Port Stanley is a lovely little beach town just south of St. Thomas with a variety of stores and restaurants. Birders will want to check out the lagoons and harbour.
  • The St. Thomas Farmer's Market takes place on Saturday mornings
  • Best coffee & pastries: The Ice Box, Streamliners Espresso Bar & Seeds Confections

London:

  • Visit either of London's food and craft markets open on weekends: Covent Garden Market and Western Fair Market
  • Try out Emily's favourite coffee shops, Black Walnut Bakery, London Bicycle Caf√©, or Edgar and Joes
