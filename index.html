Want some ideas for things to do around St. Thomas and Southern London? Here are some suggestions

  • The St. Thomas Elevated Park provides a panoramic view of the area.
  • Don't forget to check out the Jumbo the Elephant Memorial Statue!
  • Port Stanley is a lovely little beach town just south of St. Thomas with a variety of stores and restaurants. Birders will want to check out the lagoons and harbour.
  • London has two food and craft markets open on weekends: Covent Garden Market and Western Fair Market
  • The St. Thomas Farmer's Market takes place on Saturday mornings
  • Emily's favourite coffee shop has two locations in London
